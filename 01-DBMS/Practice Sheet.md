
# Questions

SELECT Basics

  1. Show all columns from the students table.
  2. Show only student_name, email, and city from students.
  3. Show all students from the city "Delhi".
  4. Show students whose cgpa is greater than 8.0.
  5. Show students admitted after the year 2021.
  6. Show all female students.
  7. Show students whose phone number is not null.
  8. Show all courses with more than 3 credits.
  9. Show instructors who joined before 2020.
  10. Show departments located in a specific building.

  Sorting and Limiting

  11. Show students ordered by cgpa from highest to lowest.
  12. Show the top 5 students by cgpa.
  13. Show courses ordered alphabetically by course_name.
  14. Show students ordered by admission_year, newest first.
  15. Show instructors ordered by joining_year, oldest first.

  Filtering

  16. Show students whose cgpa is between 7.0 and 9.0.
  17. Show students whose city is either "Delhi" or "Mumbai".
  18. Show students whose name starts with "A".
  19. Show courses where the course code contains "CS".
  20. Show enrollments where grade is not null.

  Aggregate Functions

  21. Count the total number of students.
  22. Find the average cgpa of all students.
  23. Find the highest cgpa.
  24. Find the lowest cgpa.
  25. Count how many courses exist.
  26. Count how many students are in each city.
  27. Find the average cgpa for each department.
  28. Count how many instructors are in each department.
  29. Count how many courses each department offers.
  30. Count how many enrollments exist per semester.

  JOIN Questions

  31. Show each student’s name with their department name.
  32. Show each course with its department name.
  33. Show each instructor with their department name.
  34. Show each course with its instructor name.
  35. Show each enrollment with student name and course name.
  36. Show student name, course name, semester, and grade for all enrollments.
  37. Show all students and the courses they are enrolled in.
  38. Show all courses and the instructor teaching each course.
  39. Show all students from the "Computer Science" department.
  40. Show all courses taught by a specific instructor.

  Slightly Harder Joins

  41. Show each student’s name, department, course name, and grade.
  42. Show all students enrolled in courses from their own department.
  43. Show students enrolled in courses taught by instructors from the same department.
  44. Show departments with their students, instructors, and courses.
  45. Show each instructor and the number of courses they teach.

  GROUP BY and HAVING

  46. Show departments having more than 5 students.
  47. Show courses having more than 3 enrollments.
  48. Show students enrolled in more than 1 course.
  49. Show departments where average student CGPA is greater than 8.0.
  50. Show semesters with more than 10 enrollments.

  Good Challenge Questions

  51. Find the student with the highest CGPA.
  52. Find the department with the most students.
  53. Find the course with the most enrollments.
  54. Find students who are not enrolled in any course.
  55. Find courses that have no enrollments.
  56. Find instructors who are not assigned to any course.
  57. Find students who have enrolled in more than one course.
  58. Find the average CGPA of students enrolled in each course.
  59. Find each department’s top CGPA student.
  60. Show all enrollments with student, course, instructor, and department names.
